Projects

Project 1

For the first project, we are working on implementation for the communities. This project is for the safety and well-being of residents in apartment buildings of 3, 4, 5, and 6 stories. Each floor will have several security cameras with a system for real-time monitoring and recording. To this end, on the floor with the entrance and exit, there will be a control room to manage access and supervise activities, and in case of errors, a supervisor will be notified immediately to resolve the issue.

Project 2

For the second project, we are developing a digital platform (i.e., a website) make access to services and opportunities for older people, adults, and young people.
