About Ecosystem

Ecosystem is an NGO dedicated to promoting sustainable development and cooperation, focusing on the topic of technology and its impact on older people, adults, and young people.

This NGO's project (or projects) focuses on developing technological solutions that enable older adults, adults, and young people to access services and/or opportunities effectively and sustainably. The NGO also works to digitize and improve the quality of life for community members, so that some of them can benefit from these solutions, such as requesting help or accessing information through the website, making calls with a smartphone, etc.
