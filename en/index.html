About the NGO

The objective of the NGO

The objective of this NGO is to promote sustainable development and cooperation through collaborative projects, so that communities, neighborhoods, community blocks, etc., can understand how to use technological devices, management systems, networks, etc., in a sustainable and equitable way.

In the future, the NGO plans to expand its reach and try to reach more communities to promote sustainable, cooperative development, etc.
