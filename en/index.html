Goal

The goal of this NGO is to promote sustainable development and cooperation through collaborative projects, enabling communities, neighborhoods, and other groups to understand how to use technological devices, management systems, networks, and other resources sustainably and equitably. In the future, the NGO plans to expand its reach and connect with more communities to promote sustainable and cooperative development.
